Nuffield Research Project

During the summer of 2020, I undertook a personalised project to find misidentified M-dwarfs in the GAIA survey. I was lucky enough to have Mark Gallaway as my project mentor who is an Astrophysics professor at the University of Hertfordshire Bayfordbury Observatory. I first had to filter the dataset of stars and then cross-match them with known surveys such as the Lepine surveys. Then using a JavaScript-oriented software for star plotting called TopCat, I plotted the absolute magnitude against the colour filter of the stars and compared the two distributions. The overlap between them showed the newly identified M-dwarfs which I was able to write a report about
